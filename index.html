Sonify

[Mar 2019] Developed a mobile app for Android (in Java) that visualizes musical beats through colored lights for live concerts. Sonify records a song, converts to a WAV file to extract sound energy, computes beats per minute (bpm), then flashes light accordingly. I leveraged the sound energy of a file by computing its instantaneous sound energy and calculating a rolling average energy over a window of time.
